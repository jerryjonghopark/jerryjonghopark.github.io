About Me

Update: Starting 2021, I am on a leave of absence for 3-4 years to do my mandatory military service in South Korea. Currently, as alternative service, I am working as an deep learning researcher at Krafton, led by Kangwook Lee. I expect to return for my PhD in Fall 2025.

I was an M.S. student in Computer Science at University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research interest lies in between theoretical machine learning and computational learning theory. I work on efficient algorithms for high dimensional robust statistics with Christos Tzamos and Ilias Diakonikolas. I graduated from UC Berkeley with a B.A. in Computer Science and Mathematics with high honors. At my time there, I worked with John Canny and with David Bamman.

I am grateful to be supported by Kwanjeong Educational Foundation.
