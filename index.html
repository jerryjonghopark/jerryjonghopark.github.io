About Me

I am a first year PhD student in Computer Science at UW Madison. My research interest lies in between theoretical machine learning, high dimensional statistics, and robust learning. Recently, I graduated from UC Berkeley with a B.A. in Computer Science and Mathematics with high honors. I was fortunate to work with Prof. John Canny on efficient MCMC methods and with Prof. David Bamman on event detection in NLP.

I am grateful to be supported by Kwanjeong Educational Foundation and UW Madison's CS Departmental Scholarship.
